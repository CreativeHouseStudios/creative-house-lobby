  [H1] Advo-Cassie
  [H2] A calm space for orientation and clarity
  Advo-Cassie is designed for people navigating prolonged, confusing, or
  emotionally complex situations. It offers tools for documentation, regulation,
  and steady communication without interpretation, urgency, or pressure.
  [← Back to Movements]

  WHAT THIS PROJECT IS (glassmorphism box)
  Advo-Cassie helps people stay oriented when situations feel foggy or
  overwhelming. It creates space to slow down, organize what's happening, and
  remain grounded before decisions or communication take place.

  This is not a program you commit to. It is a space you can return to when
  clarity feels difficult.

  The application experience for this project will live at advo-cassie.app
  when released.

  What this project is not:
  • Not therapy or mental health treatment
  • Not legal advice or representation
  • Not crisis intervention
  • Not a substitute for human support

  SPONSORSHIP & STEWARDSHIP (glassmorphism box)
  Advo-Cassie is being developed as a free public resource, stewarded by
  Creative House Studios.

  Sponsors make access possible by supporting:
  • original sound and music
  • survivor-informed storytelling
  • ethical design and distribution

  Sponsors do not influence content, users, or outcomes.
  Creative House curates credibility so this space remains steady, respectful,
  and safe.

  WHAT THIS IS (glassmorphism box)
  Creative House Studios actively curates and publishes creative work from people
  with a voice for good.

  We are building a social broadcast channel for films, stories, music, and books
  that carry meaning, context, and care. Some work lives here first. Some arrives
  through relationship. All of it is handled with intention.

  We curate slowly and thoughtfully. This takes time, and we will not rush it.

  WHO WE MAY WORK WITH
  If you are a filmmaker, writer, or musician:
  You may create work that is thoughtful, complex, or difficult to place in
  existing platforms.
  You may care more about who sees your work than how many people see it.
  You may value stewardship over virality.

  Left-border list:
  • Documentary films and narrative work
  • Written essays and survivor narratives
  • Music for grounding, regulation, or reflection

  OUR APPROACH
  We curate slowly and thoughtfully.
  This means:
  • We do not promise publication or outcomes
  • We maintain editorial independence
  • We prioritize dignity and context over reach

  Over time, as communities grow, some creators may choose to share their work
  through Creative House.

  This is not a submission portal.
  It is an invitation to introduce yourself.

  [label] Creative House Studios
  [H1] Systems
  Three interconnected layers that power every Creative House story universe.

  SYSTEM 01 — CREDIBILITY SPINE (sky accent)
  The data layer.
  The Credibility Spine collects, organizes, and structures stories, sources,
  research, and creator contributions into a verified database. Stories are
  mapped geographically and categorized by theme — allowing movements to
  demonstrate both reach and legitimacy.
  Details:
  • Ingests stories from multiple sources — editorial, community, research.
  • Maps stories geographically by location and impact.
  • Categorizes by theme, movement, and credibility signal.
  • Powers the Credibility Constellation visible on each Lobby.
  • Read-only access for public Lobbies — editorial control stays with the studio.

  SYSTEM 02 — LOBBY ENGINES (amber accent)
  The entry point into a storytelling universe.
  A Lobby is the cinematic entry point into a Creative House story universe.
  Visitors arrive in a designed environment where they can explore stories,
  projects, and movements. Each Lobby routes visitors into themed verticals —
  and is connected to the Credibility Spine for live story data.
  Details:
  • Cinematic hero with video background and branded identity.
  • Movement constellation — visual navigation across story verticals.
  • Live integration with the Credibility Spine.
  • Themed verticals routed by topic, geography, or community.
  • Each Lobby is independently deployable as its own domain.

  SYSTEM 03 — DISTRIBUTION SYSTEMS (rose accent)
  Automated amplification across platforms.
  Creative House builds automated distribution systems that publish and amplify
  stories across multiple channels — ensuring credible stories do not remain
  hidden. Distribution is designed to be persistent, scalable, and
  platform-agnostic.
  Details:
  • Automated publishing to YouTube, social media, and blog networks.
  • Creator network integration — distributing through trusted voices.
  • Newsletter and subscriber systems for long-term story reach.
  • Analytics integration to track story impact across channels.
  • Platform-agnostic — built to survive algorithm changes.

  THE ARCHITECTURE TOGETHER
  The three systems are designed to work as a single pipeline. A story enters
  through the Credibility Spine, is surfaced inside a Lobby Engine, and is
  distributed outward through automated channels. Each layer reinforces the
  others.

  [H1] About
  "We don't just make films. We build the systems that help stories reach
  the world."

  ABOUT THE STUDIO
  Creative House Studios is not a traditional production company and not a
  marketing agency. It is a story amplification infrastructure designed to help
  important movements, creators, and investigations reach the world.

  The studio was built around a simple observation: credible stories often fail
  not because they lack quality, but because they lack infrastructure. Creative
  House exists to fix that.

  THE CREATIVE HOUSE MISSION
  Amplify stories that matter.

  Creative House exists to amplify stories that matter and build the
  infrastructure that helps them reach the world.

  We focus on movements, investigations, and communities that already have
  credibility behind their work — and need a system to ensure that work is
  discovered, verified, and distributed at scale.

  We are not a platform. We are not an agency. We are infrastructure.

  HOW THE NETWORK WORKS
  The three-layer model.

  Every Creative House story universe is built on three interconnected layers:
  the Credibility Spine (the data layer), the Lobby Engine (the cinematic entry
  point), and the Distribution System (the amplification layer).

  Stories enter through the Credibility Spine, are surfaced through dedicated
  Lobby environments, and are distributed outward through automated multi-platform
  channels.

  This architecture ensures that every story we build for has a persistent,
  structured path to reach its intended audience — regardless of individual
  platform changes or algorithm shifts.
